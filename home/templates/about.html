ABOUT US

Who we are ?

Kavya Electrical Store is a leading brand of electrical products and accessories in India. Its range of products include modulars, switches, wires & cables, MCBs and LEDs. Established in 1988, it is today a multi-product, multi-location company with corporate office in New Delhi and multiple manufacturing facilities in the State of Uttarakhand. Over the last three decades, the high demand for our products and ever-expanding customer base helped us deliver substantial year-on-year growth and a sound balance sheet. As a result, we are proactively expanding our business with the support of leading banks and credit rating agencies in India. We continuously invest our resources in the latest equipment and technical know-how to develop high-quality products.

What we do?

Kavya Electrical Store range of products is well known for its robust quality, high tensile strength, versatile usage and efficiency. Furthermore, the conductors are insulated with a special grade of PVC for insulation as well as for outer sheath, which consists of highly abrasive resistant PVC compound. We ensure the quality of both the input material and final products through a robust quality control system. We provide value-for-money products to our customers as we specialize in sourcing high-grade raw materials at a cost-effective price.Our products come with ISI & CE quality mark and are manufactured in ISO certified facilities. We ensure that we have well equipped lab and a team highly qualified engineers who test the products as per BIS standard.
